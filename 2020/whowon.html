The 27th IOCCC Winners

Here are the names and categories for the winners of the 27th IOCCC:

  • Best one-liner
    Dave Burton (US) - Byte to binary, no loops, 1-liner
  • Best utility
    Ilya Kurdyukov (RU) - MD5 checksum
  • Most explosive
    Yusuke Endoh (JP) - Minesweeper solver
  • Most misleading indentation
    tsoj (DE) - Asteroids
  • Most head-turning
    Yusuke Endoh (JP) - ASCII clock
  • Don't tread on me award
    Cody Boone Ferguson (US) - Snake game
  • Best abuse of lámatyávë
    Ilya Kurdyukov (RU) - Markov chain text generator
  • Most enigmatic
    Cody Boone Ferguson (US) - Enigma machine
  • Most phony
    Edward Giles (AU) - DTMF encoder/decoder
  • Bset slaml prragom
    Ilya Kurdyukov (RU) - Letter permuter
  • Best perspective
    Yusuke Endoh (JP) - Star Wars crawler
  • Least detailed
    Ilya Kurdyukov (RU) - Image segmenter
  • Most percussive
    Nathan Otterness (US) - MIDI improver
  • Best abuse of CPP
    Don Yang (US) - PIN-protected program generator
  • Best of show - abuse of libc
    Nicholas Carlini (US) - printf machine
