If you want to mirror this site, especially in an area that we do not have already covered, please send EMail to Simon Cooper at:

mirror-request -at- ioccc -dot- org

We will ask you a few questions and provide you with information on how we would prefer you to mirror the site.

Please don't start mirroring until we have responded and processed your mirror request.

Thank you. We appreciate your interest in mirroring the IOCCC.
